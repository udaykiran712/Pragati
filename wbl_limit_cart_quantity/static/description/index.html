Overview

Now you can limit your customer from bulk orders of any individual product. Add checkout restrictions based on product. so that customers can only order products in a quantity specified. You can limit B2B wholesale users' product purchases to a specific quantity and amount. For example, if you've offered discounted prices to B2B user roles, you can set a minimum product, cart quantity, or amount required for checkout. You can set a limit on the number of limited edition products that can be purchased. Customers may purchase limited stock items in bulk to resell, causing them to sell out quickly. Ensure that all of your customers have the opportunity to purchase trending and best-selling limited-edition products from your store. Purchase quantities of limited stock items should be limited. When selling custom-made goods or other VIP items with limited monthly or weekly stock, you can limit how many a customer can buy in a week or month. When a customer reaches the threshold, the extension prevents them from ordering again until the next weekly or monthly period. Customers will get a clear message during the purchase of the product about the quantity restriction.
