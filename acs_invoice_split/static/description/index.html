Invoice Splitting


This Module will Add functionality of Invoice Splitting.

Split Button on Invoice Form View.
Split Invoice with two selection option: Full Invoice and Invoice Line.
In Full Invoice Splitting user can enter Percentage to split in new invoice.

In Split by "Invoice Lines" user gets two split options: Quantity and Unit Price.
In Split by Quantity User can enter qty to split.

In Split by "Invoice Lines" user gets two split options: Quantity and Unit Price.
In Split by Unit Price can enter Amount to split.
