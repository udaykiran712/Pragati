Discounts on Invoices and Bills

Discount invoices and bills in Odoo!

Discounts on Invoices and Bills add a fixed type discount for invoice and bill lines. However, by default, you can only add a percentage discount on invoices and bills in Odoo. This module allows you to add flat discounts on bills or invoices and a global discount.

Manage Discounts in Invoices in Odoo

Discounts on Invoices and Bills permit you to add a fixed discount on invoice/bill lines or entire invoice/bill (Global). Add fixed and percentage discounts on different invoice and bill order lines. Also, you can add a global discount (fixed or percentage) on the taxed or untaxed amount.

Freedom to Set Different Discount Types on Odoo Invoices and Bills

By default, Odoo only allows percentage discounts on invoice lines. And you can't add fixed/flat discounts on invoice lines.

However, our module facilitates adding flat discounts on invoices and bills in Odoo. With this Odoo app, you can add fixed and percentage discounts on Odoo invoices and bills.

You get the freedom to apply the discounts on the invoice lines or globally on the entire invoice or bill.

Moreover, the applied discounts can be on the taxed or untaxed amount on the bill lines or globally.

Add a Global Percentage or Flat Discount on the Entire Invoice

The default functionality of Odoo doesn't have the functionality to add Global Discounts on Invoices and Bills.

Besides, Odoo Discounts on Invoices and Bills module adds the feature to add a global discount on bills and invoices. Also, you can add a flat/fixed or percentage discount on the total amount of the invoice.
